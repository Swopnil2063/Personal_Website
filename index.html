Embedded Systems
Smart Irrigation System & Adaptive Light Greenhouse
My first-ever project, built in grade 11 with friends and no teacher supervision. Nepal has extensive irrigation but almost no automation, so we built a prototype on hardwood with real fertile soil and crops — a DHT sensor read soil moisture and triggered a sprinkler automatically, cutting out redundant manual work. We later extended it into an adaptive greenhouse: LED lighting adjusted to the plant's growth stage, and ventilation kicked in automatically based on internal humidity and temperature. The greenhouse itself was built from precisely measured and cut glass panels bonded with silica — getting the silica bonding right was the hardest part of the whole build.
